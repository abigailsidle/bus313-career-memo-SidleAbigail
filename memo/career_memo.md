## Career Objectives

I was promoted to in-store shopper as soon as my 3 months had elapsed. I worked as a shopper for 3 more months before being promoted to specialist (part-time management) in March. The location I worked had a total of 80-110 personnel that I got to know. During one shift, I could be managing, delegating, training, and over-seeing anywhere from 30-50 employees. I worked in this management position for 7 months before starting my education at UH Manoa at the end of August 2023.
